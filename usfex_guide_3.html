• US-FEX GUIDE
  • Invite A User
    • Open the application.
    • Log in using your “Username” and “Password”.
    • Navigate to the "Profile" section and select the "Invite User" option.
    • Click the "Invite User" button.
    • Fill in the required details on the form.
    • Click the "Invite User" button to send the invitation.
  • Broker Registration
    • After signing up as a broker, you will receive an email notification containing a registration link.
    • Open your email inbox and locate the invitation email.
    • Click on the "Registration Link" provided in the email.
    • The link will direct you to the "User Profile" page in the application.
    • Fill out the registration form with the required information and click "Next".
    • You will be redirected to the "Documents" tab.
    • Upload the contract document on the "Documents" screen. Click "Submit" to complete the process.
    • The system will then redirect you to the "Login" screen.
  • Invite Carriers to the Platform
    • As a broker, you will navigate to the "Carriers" menu and select the "Find Network Carriers" sub-tab.
    • Search for carriers using their DOT number or company name.
    • Click the "Connect" button next to the desired carrier.
    • Complete the required fields in the "Invite Carrier" pop-up.
    • Click the "Send Invitation" button.
    • The carrier will receive an email notification containing a link that directs them to the registration page.
  • Carrier Registration
    • The carrier clicks on the "Registration Link" provided in the email invitation.
    • Enter the "DOT Number" and click "Verify and Proceed".
    • Navigate to the "Profile" section and select the "Invite User" option.
    • The system navigates to the "Registration" screen.
    • Complete all the required fields on the registration screen and click "Continue".
    • The system redirects the user to the "Login" screen. Enter your credentials and click "Sign In".
    • A 2FA code will be sent to your email. Enter the code and click "Verify".
    • The system navigates to a screen with five sub-tabs:
      Equipment Types: Add the required equipment types and click "Next".
      Lanes: Add the lanes and click "Next".
      Signature: Provide your signature and click "Next".
      Documents: Upload the required documents and click "Next".
      Contract: Accept and upload the contract document, then click "Submit".
    • Click the "Invite User" button to send the invitation.
  • Brokers Creating Shipments (Bid & Contract Rates)
    • As a broker, you will navigate to the "Shipments" menu.
    • Click on the "Create Shipment" button.
    • Fill in all the required details on the "Details" screen and click "Next".
    • Complete the information on the "Contracted Rate" screen and click "Next".
    • In the "Network" screen, select the carriers from the "Visibility" section.
    • Click the "Create Shipment" button.
    • The created shipments will be listed on the "Available Loads" screen.
  • Carrier Negotiates on a Shipment
    • Broker-created shipments will be available under the "Available Loads" section for carriers.
    • Carriers navigate to the "Shipments" menu and click on "Available Loads".
    • Click the "Re-bid" button on the load card.
    • Alternatively, click the "Re-bid" button on the "Details" or "Lane Rates" screen.
    • Enter the required information in the "Re-bid" pop-up and click the "Submit Your Bid" button.
    • The updated rate will appear under the "Current Bid Rate" section.
    • To cancel the bid, click the "Cancel" button next to the "Re-bid" button.
    • To view the bid history, click the "View History" button.
    • Once the broker accepts the carrier's bid rate, the carrier clicks the "Confirm Load" button to accept the load, officially awarding it to the carrier." section.
  • Broker Negotiation on Shipment
    • As a broker, you will navigate to the "Shipments" menu and click on the "Available Loads" tab.
    • Click the "Re-bid" button on the load card.
    • Alternatively, navigate to the "Details" or "Lane Rates" screen and click the "Re-bid" button there.
    • Complete the required fields in the "Re-bid" pop-up and click "Submit Your Bid".
    • The updated rate will appear under the "Current Bid Rate" section.
    • To cancel the bid, click the "Cancel" button next to the "Re-bid" button.
    • To view the bid history, click the "View History" button.
    • The broker can accept the carrier's bid rate by clicking the "Accept" button. The status will update to "Bid Accepted".
    • Once the broker accepts the bid rate, the carrier must click the "Confirm Load" button, officially awarding the load to the carrier.
    • The awarded load will then appear under the "Shipments" menu in the "Active Shipments" sub-tab.
  • Carriers Inviting Drivers to the Platform
    • The carrier navigates to the "Driver" menu and clicks on the "Add New Driver" button.
    • Fill in the required details in the "Add New Driver" pop-up.
    • Click "Add" to send a text message to the driver with a link to download the app and register.
    • The added driver will appear under the "All Drivers" screen.
  • Carrier Assigning Shipments to Driver
    • The carrier navigates to the "Shipments" menu and clicks on the "Active Shipments" sub-tab.
    • Locate the shipment card and click the "Assign Driver" button.
    • Clicking "Assign Driver" opens a pop-up window.
    • Select a driver from the list and click the "Assign" button.
    • A confirmation pop-up will appear. Click "Yes, Assign" to finalize the assignment.
    • The driver will be notified about the assigned shipment.
